About Simple Quiz

A minimal quiz app that runs in the browser with no backend.

Features

  • Enter your name and pick a quiz
  • Timer and progress bar
  • Questions and options are shuffled
  • Auto-submit when time runs out
  • Instant score and correct/wrong summary

Files

  • index.html — Main app
  • style.css — Styles
  • quiz-data.js — Quiz content (edit to add quizzes)
  • app.js — Quiz logic
  • about.html — This page

Simple Quiz — No build, no server. Just open index.html in a browser.
